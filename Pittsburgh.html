Pittsburgh

Here are some fun facts about Pittsburgh:

  • Pittsburgh has more bridges than any city in the world!
  • The very first T-rex skeleton can be seen in person at Carnegie Museum
  • The stars in the Pittsburgh Steelers helmet logo are not actually stars, they are called hypocycloids. It was derived from the U.S. Steel, established in the city.
  • McDonald's "Big Mac" was originally invented in Pittsburgh by Jim Delligatti, and first sold in 1967.
  • The very first World Series was played in 1903, pitting the local Pittsburgh Pirates vs. The Boston Americans.
  • The Fort Pitt is the oldest structure in Pittsburgh, built back in 1764.
  • From 1891-1911, Pittsburgh was spelled with the "h" on the end.
  • Pittsburgh has more bars per person than any city in the country (12 per 10,000 residents).
  • The very first gas station in the United States was opened in 1913 in Pittsburgh.
  • Due to the limited amount of players during the 1943-1944 season, The Steelers and Eages combined to play as the Steagles.
